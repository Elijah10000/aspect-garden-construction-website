Our Services

We supply a wide variety of services here at Aspect. We can completly re-lawn your garden, to liven it up and make it green and pretty for the Summer time. If you're looking for something more substantial, we also make gorgeous, bespoke seating areas, excellently crafted and indefinatly timeless. Furthermore, we provide a more subtle look, if you're not looking to make a bold statement. We can also make plant flowerbeds or rejuvinate existing beds, general garden maintenenece, repair damaged trees, shrubs and hedges, while also building origional water features, complex patios, steps, seomras and sheds. We can also build irrigation systems, so your prized, new garden stays perfect for as long as possible. If you want it, we can build it. it's as easy as that. We want you to be happy with your garden, to show it off and turn it into the something more than just an addition to a house. We're here to make it the main attraction of where you live!

Where We Serve

Aspect serves all across Ireland. From where we're based, in Dublin, all the way to Galway, Clare and Donegal. Some of our best work has been outside of Dublin, so if you fear that it's going to be an inconvenience, fear no more! You just have to let us know 3 months in advance so we can check the site for any potential obstacles and to give us time to make mock-ups and to sort out essential man-managment. Once that is sorted out, we're good to go and planning can commence immediatly. Nobody is too far, though be warned, the further you are located, the more costly our services will be, due to transport fees and semi-perminent relocation of equipment and employees. Furthermore, if weather is too bad for work to continue, and if materials and equipment are destroyed in this process, the client must pay for repair or replacement.
